FAQ

Common questions

How much does an ADU cost?

Pricing depends on size, layout and finishes. As a guide: studios from $75,000, one-bedroom units from $110,000, two-bedroom from $155,000, and three-bedroom from $200,000 — all-inclusive and quoted with full transparency.

How long does it take to build an ADU?

Most projects move from design to move-in within several months — typically a couple of months for design and permitting, then construction. You'll have a clear schedule up front.

Do you handle ADU permits and zoning?

Yes. We research zoning, prepare and submit all drawings and applications, and coordinate with your city and county so you never get stuck fighting red tape.

What's included in your ADU pricing?

Truly turnkey: design and engineering, full construction, complete finishes, utility hookups, final inspections, and our 1-year workmanship and 10-year structural warranty.

Do you offer financing options for ADUs?

Yes — financing is available through RenoFi, so you can build now and pay over time instead of covering the full cost upfront.

Where on my property can I build an ADU?

Depending on setbacks and local zoning, an ADU can typically go in your backyard, side yard, as an attached addition, or above a garage. We confirm placement options before design begins.

Do you build ADUs on-site or off-site?

Every ADU is 100% site-built on your property to California Building Code — never a prefab kit — so it appraises like real estate and adds long-term equity.

Do ADUs increase property value?

Yes. A permitted, site-built ADU adds livable square footage and rental potential, and is treated as real estate — a strong, in-demand feature for resale.

Can I rent out my ADU legally?

In most of the Sacramento area you can legally rent a properly permitted ADU. Exact rules vary by city, and we confirm your options before you build.

What size ADU can I build?

Allowable size depends on your lot and local zoning. We confirm the exact square footage your property allows so your design uses every inch the code permits.

What areas do you serve?

We build throughout Greater Sacramento and surrounding cities within about 25 miles, including Roseville, Folsom, Rocklin, El Dorado Hills, Citrus Heights and more.

How do I get started?

Schedule a free site review. We confirm setbacks, zoning and utilities, give you a preliminary estimate, then move into design, permitting and construction.
