FAQ

Common questions

How much does an ADU cost?

Pricing depends on the model and your site. As a guide: studios start around $75,000, one-bedroom units from $110,000, and two-bedroom homes from $130,000 — all-inclusive, with no surprise add-ons.

Do you handle the permits?

Yes. We manage the entire permitting process with your city, including utilities and inspections, so you have a single point of contact from start to finish.

How long does it take to build?

Most projects move from design to move-in within a few months, depending on permitting timelines and the model you choose. We give you a clear schedule up front.

Is financing available?

Yes — financing is available through RenoFi, so you can build now and pay over time instead of covering the full cost upfront.

Which areas do you serve?

We build throughout Greater Sacramento and surrounding cities within about 25 miles, including Roseville, Folsom, Rocklin, El Dorado Hills, Citrus Heights and more.
