FAQ

Common questions

How much does an ADU cost?

Pricing depends on size, layout and finishes. As a guide: studios from $75,000, one-bedroom units from $110,000, and two-bedroom homes from $155,000 — all-inclusive and quoted with full transparency, so you know what's included from day one.

Do you handle permits and zoning?

Yes. We research zoning, prepare and submit all drawings and applications, and coordinate with your city and county — so you never get stuck fighting red tape.

How long does it take to build?

Most projects move from design to move-in within several months, depending on permitting timelines and the model you choose. You'll have a clear schedule up front.

Is financing available?

Yes — financing is available through RenoFi, so you can build now and pay over time instead of covering the full cost upfront.

Are your ADUs built on-site?

Yes. Every unit is site-built on your property to California Building Code — never a prefab kit — so it appraises like real estate and adds long-term equity.

Which areas do you serve?

We build throughout Greater Sacramento and surrounding cities within about 25 miles, including Roseville, Folsom, Rocklin, El Dorado Hills, Citrus Heights and more.
